How !ntro Works...

The process couldn't be simpler...OR MORE FUN!

Launch

Pick three photos from your Facebook, Twitter or phone to create your profile. When you go out: pick a three-word !ntro (have fun with this), select your hang-out radius and how long you'll be there, then broadcast your profile with Go !ntro

Select

This is where the fun begins! Let us know if you're interested in meeting boys, girls or both. Each profile within your selected area will flash their three pics, then you choose whether or not you like them. But be quick about it - you only see the pics for one second each!

Connect

Any two people that like each other are matched. Now you can see their profile and chat. You're already in the same area...go meet them for a drink!
